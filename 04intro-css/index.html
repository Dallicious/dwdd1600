Welcome to my audio portfolio. My name is Dallin Davis, and I am a passionate audio engineer with a knack for creating immersive soundscapes. I have honed my skills in various aspects of audio production, including sound design, mixing, mastering, and post-production.

Over the years, I have had the opportunity to work on a diverse range of projects, from podcasts and music albums to film scores and video games. Each project has allowed me to explore different facets of sound and its impact on storytelling. I have collaborated with talented artists, filmmakers, and game developers, contributing to the success of their creative endeavors with my audio expertise.

My skill set includes proficiency in industry-standard software such as Pro Tools, Ableton Live, and Logic Pro X. I am adept at Foley recording, sound synthesis, and spatial audio for VR/AR applications. My understanding of acoustics and psychoacoustics enables me to create sound that not only complements the visual element but also enhances the overall user experience.

In addition to my audio engineering work, you will find in my academic work cataloge that I have a strong passion for graphic design. This passion has allowed me to create visually compelling and meaningful designs for various projects. I am proficient in using design software like Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop, and I continuously explore new design trends and techniques to keep my work fresh and relevant. This blend of audio and visual skills enables me to offer a comprehensive package to clients, enhancing both the auditory and visual aspects of their projects.
